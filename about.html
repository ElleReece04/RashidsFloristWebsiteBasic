About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Rashid's Florist, where floral artistry meets passion. For over a decade, we have been dedicated to crafting exquisite floral arrangements that inspire and delight. Our journey began with a single vision - to bring the beauty of nature into the lives of our customers.

At Rashid's Florist, we believe that every occasion deserves the perfect touch of nature's elegance. Whether it's a romantic gesture, a celebration of love, or a momentous event, our team of skilled florists is committed to creating captivating arrangements that reflect your unique style and emotions.

Our History

Rashid's Florists is a family-owned business with a rich history spanning over 35 years. It all began in the charming town of Shallcorrss, where our founder, Rashid's parents, established a humble flower shop. With a passion for floral artistry and a deep understanding of customer needs, they laid the foundation for what would become a flourishing business.

Years later, Rashid, the son of the founders, took over the reins of the business and propelled it to new heights of success. Drawing inspiration from his family's legacy and armed with his own creative vision, Rashid expanded the business and relocated its headquarters to the vibrant city of Overport.

Today, Rashid's Florists is a renowned name in the industry, known for our impeccable craftsmanship, exceptional customer service, and an extensive selection of breathtaking floral arrangements. We continue to honor our heritage while embracing innovation, ensuring that every creation leaves a lasting impression on our valued customers.

Our Commitment

Quality and customer satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do. We source the freshest and most beautiful blooms, hand-selecting each flower to ensure their vibrancy and longevity. Our meticulous attention to detail and dedication to craftsmanship ensure that every arrangement leaving our doors is a masterpiece.

With Rashid's Florist, you can expect not only stunning floral designs but also exceptional customer service. We take pride in providing a seamless and enjoyable experience, from personalized consultations to timely delivery.
